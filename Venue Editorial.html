01
Web-based interactive navigation

Seamless navigation with interactive, web-based maps that guide visitors to their destinations using clear visual routes — smooth wayfinding in malls, campuses, and event spaces.

02
Search and Filters

Quickly find locations, services, or amenities with advanced search and category filters. Intelligent suggestions and autocomplete ensure fast, accurate results.

03
Customizable Design

Tailor the map to your brand identity with custom colors, icons, and themes. Highlight key areas, events, or promotions for a personalized, cohesive experience.

04
Landmark-Based Directions

Intuitive directions using recognizable landmarks like well-known stores or entry points — natural for first-time and returning visitors alike.

05
Continue on your Mobile

Switch devices without losing progress. Start on a kiosk and continue on mobile via QR code or link, with uninterrupted guidance through the venue.

06
Kiosk Performance Analytics

Track kiosk usage, search trends, and routing patterns with an intuitive dashboard. Real-time visualizations help optimize the visitor experience.

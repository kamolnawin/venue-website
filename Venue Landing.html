Our Key Features

Web-based interactive navigation

Provide seamless navigation with interactive, web-based maps that guide visitors to their destinations using clear visual routes. Designed for ease of use, the system ensures smooth way-finding in venues like malls, campuses, and event spaces, enhancing the overall visitor experience.

Search and Filters

Quickly find locations, services, or amenities with advanced search and category filters. Users can refine results by keywords, service types, or proximity, making discovery effortless. Intelligent suggestions and autocomplete enhance usability, ensuring fast and accurate results.

Customizable Design

Tailor the map to reflect your brand identity with custom colors, icons, and themes. Highlight key areas, events, or promotions to improve visibility and engagement, ensuring a personalized and visually cohesive wayfinding experience.

Landmark-Based Directions

Simplify navigation with intuitive, landmark-based directions. Users receive guidance using recognizable landmarks like well-known stores, or entry points, making routes easier to follow and more natural for first-time and returning visitors.

Continue on your Mobile

Seamlessly switch devices without losing navigation progress. Users can start on a desktop or kiosk and continue on mobile via QR code or link, ensuring uninterrupted guidance as they move through the venue.

Kiosk Performance Analytics

Track kiosk usage, search trends, and routing patterns with an intuitive analytics dashboard. Real-time data visualizations help optimize visitor experience, improve facility efficiency, and inform strategic decisions for better engagement and navigation.

Offline mode

Ensure users can navigate your space even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.

Cross-Platform Compatibility

A web-based solution that integrates easily with any platform, offering seamless accessibility without extra software.

Customizable Render Priority

Control the display of shop names or logos at different zoom levels, offering fine-grained visibility options.

3D render

Navigate your space with a clear, immersive 3D view for better orientation and understanding.

Accessibility Features

Offer features like wheelchair-friendly routes and multi-language options for inclusivity.
